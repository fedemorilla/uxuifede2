Analysis

In this particular task I find the best way to know the users by talking with the stockholders of the project. In this case was the Development Director who is experienced in the UX/UI field. Different will be if the stockholder is not familiar with UX process, in that case my approach will be different.

After talking a lot and making a lot of question I had a good idea of who their users were. So to solidify this this idea I start making personas and empathy map.

Personas

Name Maria Molina
Age 45
Gender Female
Job Title Mother and Small business owner
Quote “I want to know what features this app have for the security of my family”
Goals
  • Find trusty security app
  • Know if this app will satisfy my security needs.
  • See quality imaginary in the landing page to enhance my trust.
  • Be able to navigate through the landing page in my phone.
  • Easy to find Try button.
Scenario 1 Maria is talking with a friend and she tell her there is an app which can help improve the safety of their loved ones. Maria take his phone and try to find the app in Google Play and for further information she tries to find the app web page.
Scenario 2 Maria is in her desktop computer in his house. She accesses to Facebook and find an add suggestion of a security app for the loved ones. She access the landing page and search what this app have to offer.
Name Carlos Bustamante
Age 51
Gender Male
Job Title Father and small business owner
Quote “Find a convincing app that can offer real solutions of security to my family”
Goals
  • URL easy to write
  • See convincing features
  • Easy to see Try button to see the app.
  • See quality imaginary in the landing page to enhance my trust.
  • Be able to navigate through the landing page in my phone.
Scenario 1 Carlos is in his office and a college show him Buddy 4 Life app and tell him about the app features. Carlos is interested so he go to his desktop Pc and try to find more information about the app.
Scenario 2 Carlos know there are some app that can offer security services and he navigate through google Play to find the options available. He finds Buddy 4 Life app and download it and start using it. To know the complete features he try to find Buddy 4 Life web page.

Empathy Map

Continuing with the objective of solidify and making more tight the landing page users I find useful making an empathy map.

Name Maria Molina
Age 45
Gender Female
Thinks and feels:
  • There is a lot of crime.
  • There are no enough police force in my neighborhood.
  • At night is the most dangerous moment to come back home.
  • I´m fear someone kidnap my kids.
  • Where is my son right now?
Hear:
  • We are in dangerous time, there is a lot of crime.
  • TV: another kidnap and rape crime.
  • Your daughter must have careful with who chat in social media.
Say and do:
  • Do you know if my son come back home?
  • Is there anyone with you to accompany you?
  • What time you come back?
  • Don´t go out tonight, is dangerous.
  • I going to call Martha if he know where is Steve.
Gain
  • He is happy wen find out his kids are all right.
  • Feels secure if he know where are their loves ones.
  • Relieved knowing their loves one are ok.
Pain
  • Having no idea where his kids are.
  • Feeling insecure wen she is out.
  • Sating home fearless to go out.
